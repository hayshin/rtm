\caption{Overview of the proposed automated pipeline for mapping unstructured
clinical audio to HL7 FHIR-compliant data structures. The pipeline comprises
six sequential stages:
(1)~raw clinical audio input,
(2)~automatic speech recognition via Whisper~\cite{radford2022whisper},
(3)~LLM-based post-processing for transcription refinement~\cite{singh2024sound},
(4)~clinical NLP for named entity recognition and relation extraction,
(5)~terminology normalization against standard clinical vocabularies
(SNOMED~CT, LOINC, RxNorm),
and (6)~generation of validated HL7 FHIR resource instances~\cite{bender2021fhir}.}
\label{fig:pipeline}
